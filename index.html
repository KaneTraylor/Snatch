What makes up your credit score?

Here are some ways that Snatch can target specific needs for your credit health

35% Payment history

This is your on time payments with cards or accounts.

30% Credit utilization

This is the amount you've used, of your total credit limit. We help to keep utilization rates at 1%

15% Age of accounts

This is the total amount of time you've had established history.
